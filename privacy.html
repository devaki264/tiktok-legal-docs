Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 3, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to TikTok Creator Analyzer ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we collect, use, and safeguard your information. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data when you use our application that integrates with TikTok.

2. Information We Collect

When you connect your TikTok account to our application, we collect:

  • TikTok Profile Information: Username, display name, profile picture, bio description, follower count, following count, and video count
  • Public Video Data: Video IDs, descriptions, titles, thumbnails, creation dates, and publicly available engagement metrics (likes, comments, shares, views)
  • Video Content: Video descriptions and metadata for analysis purposes
  • Authentication Data: Access tokens and refresh tokens provided by TikTok OAuth (stored securely)

Important: We only access public information that you explicitly authorize us to access through TikTok's OAuth authentication. We do NOT collect:

  • Private or draft videos
  • Direct messages or private communications
  • Payment or financial information
  • Personal contact details beyond what's public on your TikTok profile

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Content Analysis: Analyze your public videos using artificial intelligence (Google Gemini AI) to classify content categories and assess content reliability for fact-based videos
  • Engagement Metrics: Calculate and display average engagement rates based on your public video statistics
  • Creator Profiles: Create comprehensive creator profiles to help businesses identify suitable partnership opportunities
  • Platform Matching: Connect content creators with relevant business opportunities based on content categories and reliability scores
  • Service Improvement: Improve our algorithms and analysis accuracy
  • Authentication: Maintain your session and provide secure access to our services

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on Google Cloud Platform (GCP) infrastructure with the following security measures:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted at rest and in transit using industry-standard encryption protocols
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls limit who can access your data
  • Secure Authentication: OAuth tokens are stored in Google Cloud Secret Manager
  • Regular Backups: Data is regularly backed up to prevent loss
  • Compliance: Our infrastructure complies with industry security standards

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate our application:

  • TikTok API: To authenticate users and access public TikTok data (subject to TikTok's Privacy Policy)
  • Google Cloud Platform: For secure data storage, processing, and hosting
  • Google Gemini AI: For content analysis and categorization (text analysis only, no personal data shared)
  • Google Speech-to-Text: For transcribing video audio when necessary for analysis

Each of these services has their own privacy policies. We ensure all third-party services meet appropriate privacy and security standards.

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell your personal data to third parties.

We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly agree to share your creator profile with businesses for partnership opportunities
  • Aggregated Data: We may share anonymized, aggregated statistics that cannot identify you individually
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government regulation
  • Service Providers: With trusted service providers (like Google Cloud) who assist in operating our service, under strict confidentiality agreements

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request access to the data we have collected about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data from our systems
  • Revoke Access: Disconnect your TikTok account at any time through TikTok's app settings or by contacting us
  • Export: Request a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Object: Object to certain processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services. When you disconnect your TikTok account or request data deletion:

  • OAuth tokens are immediately revoked and deleted
  • Personal data is deleted within 30 days
  • Aggregated, anonymized data may be retained for analytical purposes
  • We may retain certain data longer if required by law

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately, and we will delete such information.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure that such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws and that your data receives adequate protection wherever it is processed.

11. Cookies and Tracking

We use essential cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Maintain your login session
  • Remember your preferences
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud
  • Analyze how our service is used (anonymized)

You can control cookies through your browser settings, but disabling certain cookies may affect functionality.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Sending you a notification if you have an active account
  • Posting a notice on our application

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

Response Time: We aim to respond to all inquiries within 48 hours

For European Users (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.
